• Travis Pastrana

    #199
    Travis Alan Pastrana is an American professional motorsports competitor and stunt performer who has won championships and X Games gold medals in several disciplines, including supercross, motocross, freestyle motocross, and rally racing. He runs a show called Nitro Circus, and has competed in the Global RallyCross Championship and the Monster Jam circuit. Pastrana replicated three of daredevil Evel Knievel's most famous jumps without injury in record-breaking fashion while in Las Vegas.
